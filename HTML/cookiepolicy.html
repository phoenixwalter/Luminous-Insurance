Cookies

This Cookie Policy elucidates the operations of the website by Luminous Insurance ("we," "us," and "our"). The website employs cookies and additional tools to discern you (“user”, “visitor”, “you” or “your”) from other users, facilitating an enhanced user experience and enabling continuous improvement of the website at https://www.luminousinsurance.com. Your continued use of the website implies your consent for us to place cookies on your computer. For further details, please review our comprehensive Cookie Policy.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a compact data file that your browser receives and stores. The information in the cookie enables a website to identify a specific browser installation, facilitating the transmission of personalized information to that installation.

Why do we use Cookies?

At Luminous Insurance, cookies are essential for smooth navigation, recognizing returning visitors, and maintaining page continuity. They analyze traffic patterns, improve site functionality, and deliver targeted content based on user behavior. We prioritize transparency and security, providing detailed information about the types and purposes of cookies we use.

  • Essential Cookies

    Essential cookies play a critical role in maintaining the core functionality of the site. Without the presence of essential cookies, key features pivotal to our online services, including secure session management and user logins, would cease to operate effectively. These cookies are fundamental in ensuring the reliability and security of our digital interactions with clients, guaranteeing a streamlined and protected online experience for all users.

  • Performance Cookies

    Performance cookies are integral to the optimization of our site by collecting valuable data on user interactions, such as frequently visited pages and error messages. These cookies empower us to enhance the overall user experience. Performance cookies assist us in identifying areas for improvement, refining site performance, and ensuring that our digital platform operates at its best.

  • Functional Cookies

    Functional cookies are tailored to enhance user convenience on our Luminous platform. These cookies retain user preferences, such as our web chat service or customized display options, across multiple visits. This ensures a consistent and personalized experience for each of our users, eliminating the need for repetitive adjustments. Functional cookies contribute to a user-centric interface, providing a smoother and more enjoyable navigation process.

  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies

    Targeting or advertising cookies are employed to deliver relevant content for our customers based on your browsing habits and history. These cookies are held by trusted third-party advertisers, which enable us to personalize the advertising experience of our customers.

Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of essential, performance, functional, and targeting/advertising cookies as described in this policy. You can modify your cookie preferences through the settings on your browser or on our site.

How to manage Cookies?

Managing cookies on our website is simple. Adjust preferences through your browser settings or use our cookie setting. You can accept, decline, or delete some or all cookies as your preference. Please be aware that blocking essential cookies may impact core features.

Updates to the Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our cookie usage. Please review this policy periodically for any modifications.
