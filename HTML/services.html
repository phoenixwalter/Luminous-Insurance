Insurance products

Home Insurance

Securing your home is undoubtedly important. Our Luminous Home insurance policy brings you peace of mind by providing reassurance by offering coverage for various unexpected events that could impact your home and belongings.

Fire Insurance

Fire is used everywhere, therefore preventing the danger and risks caused by the fire is necessary. At Luminous, Fire insurance covers the damages and losses caused to a residential or business property due to fire.

Health Insurance

Protecting your health is first and foremost, as unexpected medical issues can arise at any time, necessitating potentially high-cost healthcare. With Luminous, we assured you that you don't need to be concerned about the financial burden of medical expenses, as we provide comprehensive coverage for your treatment fees.

Life insurance

Life is unpredictable, and the future is uncertain. This is why life insurance is an essential aspect of financial planning, as we provide consolation for you and your loved ones in the event of unforeseen circumstances. Luminous Life insurance policy can cover funeral expenses, pay off debts, and provide financial stability for your family when you’re no longer around.

Disability insurance

Unexpected circumstances always happened suddenly, if you cannot work and earn an income because of an illness or injury. Don't worry, Our Luminous insurance company provides Disability insurance that helps protect you and your family from the burden of financial protection caused by an unexpected illness or accident.

Auto insurance

In today's technologically advanced world, owning a vehicle is an integral part of our daily life. However, unforeseen accidents can happen anytime, anywhere, and to anyone. Underscoring the crucial importance of auto insurance. As a safeguard against unexpected events, we provide financial protection and peace of mind for car owners.

Travel Insurance

Anyone can't guarantee that we won't have any loss during traveling. Our Luminous Travel insurance can cover back your losses during your travel. Depending on the coverage you choose, travel insurance can cover a broad array of possible damages and losses like injury or sickness, losting luggage, last-minute cancellations and coverage beyond your credit card.

Pet insurance

Problems relating with your beloved pets could cause a long time of worrying, therefore it's necessary to take care of your lovely pets. Our Luminous Pet insurance mainly provides cover for veterinary fees if your pet is injured or becomes ill and is a sensible approach to assuring this does not become a concern.

Education Insurance

Education is one of the most important things in our lives, therefore it's also one of the main concerns for parents and their children. What happens if your parents are no longer able to support you financially? These are just some of the reasons education insurance is a significant investment. Keeping an Education from Luminous can provide coverage for the cost of tuition and related expenses for you or your child.Luminous can provide coverage for the cost of tuition and related expenses for you or your child.
