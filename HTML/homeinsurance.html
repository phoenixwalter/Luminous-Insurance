Home Insurance

Securing your home is undoubtedly important. Our Luminous Home insurance policy brings you peace of mind by providing reassurance by offering coverage for various unexpected events that could impact your home and belongings.

Coverage Duration: 1 Year


Premium amount: Approximately 1,000,000 MMK to 5,000,000 MMK (Monthly)


Policy Duration: We offer appropriate coverage for designated medical treatment expenses, and in the unfortunate events of Total Permanent Disability (TPD) or the policyholder's death, we provide Lump Sum Payment.
