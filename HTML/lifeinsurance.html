Life Insurance

Life is unpredictable, and the future is uncertain. This is why life insurance is an essential aspect of financial planning, as we provide consolation for you and your loved ones in the event of unforeseen circumstances. Luminous Life insurance policy can cover funeral expenses, pay off debts, and provide financial stability for your family when you’re no longer around.

Policy Duration: 10 Years (or) 20 years


Premium amount: Approximately, 10,000,000 MMK to 50,000,000 MMK (Annually)

Benefit amount: You have the flexibility to choose any desired coverage amount for the death benefit when purchasing our life insurance policy.

Life Insurance Benefits

No. Benefit Types Inclusions Benefit Amount
1 Maturity Benefit - Premium refund
2 Death Benefit (The pre-selected amount when purchasing the policy) Lump-sum payment
3 Terminal illness Benefit
(Expectancy >12 months)
  • Organ Failure
  • Last stage of Cancer
  • Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)
 50% of the Death Benefit
(Full death benefit will be paid out after the passing)

Hazardous activity >> Any circumstances resulting from the participation in hazardous activities (intendedly) are not valid for any coverage amount from us.
The benefit amount will be paid-out if you’ve been informed about risky hobbies before purchasing our policy.

Consumption of Intoxicating substances >> We do not reimburse medical expenses associated with the use of intoxicating substances, including alcohol (if not pre-specified) or drugs.
Please specify your lifestyle habits in our questionnaire form, to receive appropriate coverage for drinking alcohol or smoking.

Suicide>> The Benefit amount for the suicide or any attempts in the first 2 years after purchasing our Life Insurance, will not be paid.

The pre-determined amount of Death will be pay-out to the beneficiaries in the event of the insured person’s death.

To qualify for Life Insurance coverage, certain prerequisites must be met.

  • Please provide well-captured images of your birth certificate and national identification card (front and back).
  • Additionally, submit PDF files containing records from your significant past medical treatments,
    blood test results, and urinalysis results.
  • Lastly, complete our questionnaire form regarding your Personal details, Life-Style habits, Occupation, Financial information and Risky hobbies.
