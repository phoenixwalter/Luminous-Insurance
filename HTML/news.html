2023: Donation to Victims of Cyclone Mocha


Luminous recently donated 8,000,000 MMK to the victims of the Mocha crisis and a rescue organization, showcasing our commitment to social responsibility. This philanthropic gesture aims to provide crucial relief and support during challenging times, emphasizing our dedication to creating a positive impact on individuals and communities.

2020: Donation for Coivd-19


In a demonstration of our unwavering commitment to community well-being, we proudly announce our support for healthcare centers through a generous donation of 10,000,000 Myanmar Kyats. This contribution is specifically earmarked for addressing the urgent needs of both Covid-19 victims and the essential requirements of healthcare facilities. By extending our helping hand in this critical time, we aim to fortify the resilience of our healthcare infrastructure and contribute meaningfully to the fight against the ongoing challenges posed by the pandemic.

2018: Commemoration for donation


In commemorating the 10th anniversary of our inaugural donation, we took a meaningful step to give back to the community by providing sustenance to Sa-tu-di-thar for the entire quarter. This initiative not only marks a decade of our dedicated philanthropic efforts but also serves as a testament to our enduring commitment to uplifting the lives of those in need. By extending our support in this manner, we aim to nurture a spirit of solidarity and compassion, echoing the values that have guided us since our very first donation a decade ago.

2017: Supporting for H1N1 victims


In a steadfast commitment to community well-being, Luminous continues its unwavering support for healthcare centers during the H1N1 pandemic. Concurrently, we joyously celebrated the 20-year milestone of establishing health insurance and forging partnerships with two esteemed hospitals. This dual initiative not only reflects our enduring dedication to public health but also commemorates two decades of fostering strong alliances and promoting comprehensive healthcare coverage

2016: Donation for foundation of stray animals


In celebration of reaching a milestone of 1,000 insured , Luminous proudly extended its philanthropic endeavors by making a heartfelt donation to the Foundation for Caring for Stray Animals. This initiative not only marks our commitment to the well-being of pets but also underscores our dedication to supporting organizations that advocate for the welfare of stray animals in our community.

2015: Donation to free education centers


In a thoughtful and philanthropic gesture, Luminous Insurance Company commemorated the establishment of education insurance by generously donating 5,000,000 Myanmar Kyats to free education centers. This significant contribution not only underscores our commitment to promoting education but also exemplifies our dedication to supporting initiatives that empower and uplift communities through accessible and quality learning opportunities.

2013: Involving in planting campaign


In an active participation towards fostering a clean and green environment, Luminous enthusiastically engages in a planting industry campaign. With a steadfast commitment to environmental sustainability, we contribute a substantial support of 3,000,000 Myanmar Kyats to ensure the success of this season's initiative. This endeavor exemplifies our dedication to promoting eco-friendly practices and creating a lasting positive impact on the environment.

2013: Partnership with Phoenix Electronic Company


Mrs. Andrew, CEO of Phoenix Electronic Company, has reached out to Luminous Insurance Company for a potential partnership. This promising collaboration aims to combine strengths and expertise for mutual benefit and innovative ventures. We anticipate a fruitful alliance between our organizations.

2010: Donation to Orphanages and Nursing houses


In a heartfelt display of social responsibility, Luminous Insurance Company recently made a compassionate contribution by donating 2,500,000 MMK to support orphanages and an additional 2,500,000 MMK to aid nursing houses in North Dagon, Yangon. This dual act of philanthropy reflects our commitment to making a positive impact on diverse communities, particularly those in need, as we strive to foster a more caring and supportive society.

2008: Ribbon Cutting Ceremony


Luminous Company celebrated the commencement of a valuable partnership with ----- Clinic for pet insurance with a ribbon-cutting ceremony. As part of the festivities, we also hosted a meal for quarters and guests, emphasizing our commitment to fostering a healthy and supportive community.

2008: Donation to victims of Cyclone Nargis


In a poignant first act of philanthropy since our insurance company's establishment 16 years ago, Luminous has donated 2,000,000 MMK to both Nargis cyclone victims and a rescue organization. This marks the beginning of our commitment to making meaningful contributions in times of crisis.
