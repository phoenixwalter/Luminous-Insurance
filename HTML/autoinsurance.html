Auto Insurance

In today's technologically advanced world, owning a vehicle is an integral part of our daily life. However, unforeseen accidents can happen anytime, anywhere, and to anyone. Underscoring the crucial importance of auto insurance. As a safeguard against unexpected events, we provide financial protection and peace of mind for car owners.

Policy Duration: 6 Months (or) 1 Year


Coverage Types: Liability coverage, Collision Coverage, Comprehensive coverage

Premium amount: Approximately, 250,000 MMK to 2,100,000 MMK (Monthly)
3,000,000 MMK to 25,000,000 MMK (Annually)

Benefit amount: We provide various types of coverage as your preference to shield any unexpected accidents occurring relating to your vehicle, the benefit amount could be differentiated.

Intentional Acts >> We do not pay any coverage for collisions or damages resulting intentionally by the insured.

Driving Under the Influence >> We do not reimburse expenses for any accidents, damages or injuries
caused under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Excluded Drivers >> In the event of unstated driver in the policy gets into the accident while driving the insured vehicle,
we will not pay for any coverages.
Please provide additional information about the secondary drivers in the enrollment form.

In the event of accidents or damage relating to the insured vehicle, we will provide coverage according to the respective coverage type you’ve purchased.

To qualify for Auto Insurance coverage, certain prerequisites must be met.

  • Please provide well-captured images of your birth certificate, national identification card, and driver's licenses of both primary driver and secondary driver if necessary. (front and back).
  • Then, please submit photographs of the vehicle, ensuring that all sides are captured: front, rear, left, and right.
  • Additionally, submit documentation including the age, type, brand, and value of the vehicle you wish to be insured.
  • Lastly, complete our questionnaire form detailing the usage of the vehicle and safety features.
